How does Efiwe work?

Effiwe works in three easy steps
1: a book or group of books are donated
2: we wrap the books indvidually then place them into box for shipping
3:The books are shipped and delivered to happy students in Nigeria

Who usually donates?

We get our books mainly through student donation, university assistance. They help us by donating books of the collegic level to send to Nigeria to help better improve an create, a more beneficial educational environment.

We first examine all the books to make sure they are ready to ship, the books which are ready to ship will be wrapped, then placed into a shipping container, each packaged shipped will head directly to a school or instituion in need. Which in turn will be given out to the students, so they may broaden there horizon.

Volunteers are the most essectinal part to what effiwe is. This website was made by volunteers, the founder is a volunteer, the people who prepare for shipping are volunteers; even the book drive orgranizers, and our book distributors are volunteers. Volunteers are is the most central point to our organization so please read more and see what you can do.

We get our books from either book donations or book drives started and ran through volunteers. Our book donations mainly come through school and students, with the ocasional text book publisher, or some other corpral donation.
